About Me

My name is Sosa Esezobor. I grew up in a big city, Lagos, Nigeria. In August 2024, I moved from my home in Nigeria to Las Cruces, New Mexico to begin my masters program in Rhetoric & Professional Communication at the NMSU English Department. I also work as a teaching assistant where I teach Technical & Professional Communication. When I am not teaching or attending classes, I coordinate activities and mentor tutors at the University Writing Center. My research interests include UX Research & Design, sound politics and closed captioning. I am also a digital content creator and beauty enthusiast and I create makeup tutorials & videos on social media for fun!
